# 🌵 Cactus Logistics

### Project Overview
Cactus Logistics is a high-integrity, modular platform designed to scale across the entire logistics lifecycle. We provide innovative fulfillment and logistics solutions tailored for **SMB, Mid-Market, and Enterprise-level clients**. Our architectural mission is to evolve from a high-performance **Rating Engine** into a comprehensive **Billing Suite**, and ultimately a full-scale **Warehouse Management System (WMS)**.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Roadmap & Market Vision

### Market Scale
- **SMB & Mid-Market:** Streamlined, self-service tools for rapid growth.
- **Enterprise:** Robust, high-volume APIs, advanced auditing, and multi-tenant scalability.

### Product Evolution
1. **Phase 1: Core Rating Engine (Current)**
   - Multi-carrier rate lookups and real-time quote generation.
2. **Phase 2: Billing & Financial Suite**
   - Automated invoicing, audit trails, and secure financial integrations.
3. **Phase 3: Full Warehouse Management (WMS)**
   - Inventory "stock truth," pick-path optimization, and floor execution tools.

---

## 🛠 2026 "Gold Standard" Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** Next.js 16 (App Router) for an elite, fast UI.
* **Language:** TypeScript for enterprise-grade integrity and type safety.
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui for modern, innovative design.
* **Database & Auth:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) for secure, relational architecture.
* **Real-time:** WebSockets for live shipment and warehouse updates.
